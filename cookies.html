What Are Cookies?

This website uses minimal cookies solely for basic functionality. Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help websites function properly.

Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies for site navigation (if applicable)
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use advertising or analytics cookies
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We do not allow external companies to track you

What We Don't Use

  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking
  • Analytics that identify you
  • Marketing or behavioral tracking

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Since we use very few cookies, this should not significantly affect your experience on this website.

To disable cookies:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
